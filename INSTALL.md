
## 📱 Android - Easiest Method (PWA Install)

**No download. No settings changes. No permissions needed.**

1. Open **Chrome** on your Android phone
2. Visit **[ssec-seo.vercel.app](https://ssec-seo.vercel.app)**
3. You'll see a banner at the bottom: **"Install ssec-seo Scanner"**
4. Tap **"Install"**
5. The app installs automatically and appears on your home screen!

✅ No "Unknown Sources" needed  
✅ No APK download required  
✅ Updates automatically when you visit the site
