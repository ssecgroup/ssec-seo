# Privacy Policy for ssec-seo

**Last Updated: April 10, 2026**

## Overview
ssec-seo is a free, open-source SEO scanner browser extension. We respect your privacy.

## Data Collection
- **We DO NOT collect any personal information.**
- **We DO NOT track your browsing history.**
- **We DO NOT store any user data on external servers.**

## What the Extension Does
- Reads the URL of your current tab **only when you click the extension icon**.
- Sends the URL to our API at `ssec-seo.vercel.app` for SEO analysis.
- Displays the results in the extension popup.

## Third-Party Services
- API calls are made to `https://ssec-seo.vercel.app/api/scan`
- No third-party analytics or tracking services are used.

## Data Security
- All API communication uses HTTPS encryption.
- No user data is stored or logged on our servers.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here.
